# Elevate Pediatric Therapy Website - Design Brainstorm

## Three Approaches

### 1. "Golden Bubbles" — Whimsical Warmth
A warm, playful design where floating golden bubbles create a sense of joy and lightness. Cream backgrounds with golden accents, organic shapes, and a friendly editorial tone.

## Chosen Approach: "Golden Bubbles" — Whimsical Warmth

This approach best matches Amy's personality (warm, approachable, playful) and aligns with the reference site she loves (Binstock Sprouts) while incorporating her signature bubble theme and gold branding.

### Design Movement
Organic Modernism meets Editorial Warmth — inspired by contemporary wellness brands that balance professionalism with approachability through organic shapes, warm tones, and editorial typography.

### Core Principles
1. **Warmth First** — Every element should feel inviting, like a warm hug. No cold, clinical aesthetics.
2. **Playful Sophistication** — Bubbles and organic shapes add whimsy without feeling childish or unprofessional.
3. **Breathing Room** — Generous whitespace lets content breathe and creates a calm, unhurried feeling.
4. **Personal Connection** — The design should feel like Amy is speaking directly to the parent, not a faceless corporation.

### Color Philosophy
The palette is rooted in **golden warmth** — the color of sunshine, optimism, and nurturing energy. Gold represents the "elevated" quality of care while remaining approachable.

### Layout Paradigm
**Asymmetric Editorial Flow** — inspired by Binstock Sprouts. The page flows as a single continuous scroll with distinct sections separated by organic bubble-themed dividers. Content alternates between left-aligned and right-aligned compositions to create visual rhythm.

### Signature Elements
1. **Floating Bubble Decorations** — Semi-transparent golden circles of varying sizes that float around sections, creating depth and playfulness.
2. **Organic Wave Dividers** — Soft, bubbly wave shapes between sections that echo the roundness of bubbles.
3. **Handwritten Accent Text** — A script/handwritten font used sparingly for personal touches like "hey, I'm Amy" or section labels.

### Interaction Philosophy
Interactions should feel **gentle and delightful** — like watching bubbles float:
- Bubbles gently drift on scroll (parallax)
- Buttons have soft scale-up on hover with a golden glow
- Sections fade in softly as they enter viewport
- Service cards lift slightly on hover with a subtle shadow

### Animation
- **Floating bubbles:** CSS keyframe animations with gentle up/down drift (8-15s cycles)
- **Scroll reveals:** Elements fade-in + translate-up (20px) with staggered delays
- **Hover states:** 200ms ease transitions, scale(1.02) on cards, golden glow on buttons
- **Page load:** Hero content fades in sequentially (title → subtitle → CTA)
