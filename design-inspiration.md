# Design Inspiration Notes

## Binstock Sprouts (binstocksprouts.com) - FAVORITE
- **Layout:** Single-page landing with sections flowing vertically
- **Color palette:** Warm cream/beige background, olive/sage green accents, lavender/periwinkle sections, golden/olive CTA buttons
- **Typography:** Mix of elegant serif headings (large, editorial) + clean sans-serif body text
- **Structure:** 
  - Top nav: ABOUT | SERVICES | [LOGO] | INFO | BLOG | CONTACT
  - Hero: Large heading + subtitle + CTA on right side, clean whitespace
  - Intro section with personal greeting
  - Services section (lavender bg, wavy divider) with service cards
  - Marquee/ticker text strip
  - Personal "I'm glad you stopped by" section
  - About section: photo on left, bio text on right with green bg
  - CTA section with pattern background
  - Footer with links + social
- **Design elements:** Wavy/organic dividers between sections, decorative patterns, large photos, editorial feel
- **Vibe:** Warm, welcoming, professional but approachable, editorial/magazine-like

## Key Takeaways for Elevate Pediatric Therapy:
1. Single-page landing with smooth scroll sections
2. Warm, inviting color palette (gold/yellow for Amy vs green for Eliza)
3. Mix of serif + sans-serif typography for editorial feel
4. Organic/wavy section dividers (we'll use bubble-themed dividers)
5. Personal, conversational tone in copy
6. Large hero with tagline + CTA
7. Services section with clear descriptions
8. Personal about section with photo placeholder
9. Contact CTA section
10. Clean footer with social links
