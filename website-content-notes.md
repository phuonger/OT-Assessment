## Services & Specialties
- **Service Type:** Occupational Therapy (OT)
- **Age Group:** 0-3 years (early intervention)
- **Service Ratio:** One therapist : one client
- **Delivery Methods:** In-Home, In Clinic, Tele-Health
- **Languages:** English, Vietnamese, Spanish
- **Office Hours:** 8:30-5:00pm
- **Session Length:** 1 hour (45-50 min in-person, 10-15 min documentation)

### Specialty Areas
- Feeding
- Sensory Processing
- Neurodevelopmental
- Solid Starts (pro-member)
- Primitive Reflex Integration

### Assessments Used
- Developmental Assessment of Young Children-2 (DAYC-2)
- Bayley Scales of Infant Development IV (BSID-IV)
- Receptive-Expressive Emergent Language Test 3rd edition (REEL)
- Sensory Profile - 2

### Common Goals Worked On
- Self-feeding skills: bottle feeding, transition to solids, using utensils, straw/open cup, etc
- Dressing: Putting on socks, shirts, and pants independently
- Fine motor skills: bilateral coordination, grasp, hand strength, finger isolation, etc.
- Sensory processing skills: motor planning, body awareness, modulation, discrimination

## Mission/Philosophy
- "Play is the most important activity in a child's daily life – children learn to walk, talk, eat, and explore their environment through play."
- "At Elevate Pediatric Therapy, we aim for 'taking play to new heights' by empowering the family's understanding of play to help their child thrive and develop the essential skills needed for a bright future."
- "Occupational therapy is rooted in self-care skills, so helping caregivers understand purposeful play methods to improve their self-care skills and developmental milestones is very important to us."
- "We are committed to staying up-to-date with the latest research and evidence-based practices"
- "We are passionate about educating through play and using simple and affordable techniques that parents can easily access and implement at home."
